About Us

A serious, responsible space for defence-tech learning and early-stage R&D—structured like a company, built like a lab.

What we are

Yuiitsre Defence & Research is an MSME-registered, student-led research initiative. We focus on software-centric defence concepts, simulation environments, secure system design, and responsible exploration of autonomous technologies.

What we are not

We do not manufacture weapons. We do not claim military capability or government contracts. We build knowledge, prototypes, and a learning environment with ethics and safety as the baseline.
